Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

A simple but astounding peanut butter sandwich. Great for a late night snack or quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon, to taste
  • 4 tbsp raisins
  • 8 slices wholegrain bread

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix together peanut butter, honey, and cinnamon.
  2. Spread about 2 tablespoons of the peanut butter spread over one side of one slice of bread. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon raisins evenly over peanut butter, and place a slice of bread on top of the raisins to make a sandwich. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
